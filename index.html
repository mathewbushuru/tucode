Projects and resources

Projects

Our easy to follow, step-by-step guides encourage students to learn Scratch, HTML/CSS, and Python while creating their own games, animations and websites.


Resources

We provide additional resources for our clubs, such as a welcome pack to help you get started and downloadable certificates. We also run regular competitions and giveaways.




It's easy to start a coding club in your school

Anyone (teacher or student) can run a code club : you don't need previous coding experience or any special equipment. Use our free resources and any computers in your school to get started. We will support you along the way.

We hold regular events for our member clubs. Some events are dedicated to training by people in the industry while others are competition-style events for you to test the skills you have learnt.

Technology has a language. It's called code. And we believe coding is an essential skill. Learning how to code teaches you how to solve problems and work together in creative ways. So we've designed a new approach to coding that let's anyone learn, write, and teach it.
